# Water

## Description

Water encompasses the coastline, the marine environment including marine navigation; also waterways and water management.

## Classification

Water features in OpenStreetMap are organized into overlapping categories:

- **Coastline** - Features along the sea's edge
- **Marine** - Offshore features including drilling platforms, conservation areas, islands, and shipping routes
- **Waterways** - All watercourses: rivers, streams, waterfalls, lakes, reservoirs, and man-made channels
- **Water Management** - Human water infrastructure: reservoirs, dams, pipelines, wells, fountains, drinking points
