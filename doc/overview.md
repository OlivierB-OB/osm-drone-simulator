# Drone Simulator - Overview

## What is the Drone Simulator?

The Drone Simulator is an interactive web application that lets you pilot a virtual drone over real-world terrain. It combines actual elevation data with an intuitive flight control system, allowing you to explore landscapes from a drone's perspective in real time.

## Key Features

- **Real-World Terrain** - Fly over actual geographic locations with accurate elevation data from satellite imagery
- **Interactive Flight Control** - Pilot the drone using simple keyboard controls (arrow keys for movement, intuitive heading control)
- **Chase Camera View** - Follow the drone from behind with a dynamic camera that adjusts as you fly
- **Smooth Navigation** - Seamless terrain loading as you move, with no interruption to your flight
- **Responsive Controls** - Frame-rate independent physics ensures consistent flight behavior regardless of your device's performance

## Use Cases

- **Exploration** - Virtually visit and explore geographic locations from a drone's perspective
- **Learning** - Understand terrain topology and geography through interactive 3D visualization
- **Flight Simulation** - Practice drone piloting and navigation with realistic controls
- **Geographic Visualization** - Visualize elevation data and landscape features in an intuitive 3D environment

## How It Works

The simulator loads terrain data for your current location and dynamically updates it as you move. The 3D view follows your drone, giving you a pilot's-eye perspective of the landscape. All elevation data is sourced from real-world geographic data, ensuring the terrain you see is accurate.
