# Key: `craft` — Craft

> Source: https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:craft

## Description

The `craft=*` key is used for a place producing or processing customised goods. Unlike `shop=*` (retail trade focused on selling pre-produced goods) or `man_made=works` (factories and industrial facilities), craft locations produce goods on demand with customisation to the customer's needs. The tag applies to workshops, ateliers, and trade offices.
