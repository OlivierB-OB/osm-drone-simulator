# Key: `healthcare` — Healthcare Facilities

> Source: https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:healthcare

## Description

Used to tag healthcare facilities, practitioners, and services. The key applies to nodes and areas (including multipolygon relations), but not to ways or standard relations. Status: approved.

## Values

### `healthcare=alternative`

- **Element types**: node / area
- **Description**: An alternative or complementary medicine practitioner or facility, covering practices such as homeopathy, naturopathy, or traditional medicine outside conventional healthcare.
- **Status**: in use
