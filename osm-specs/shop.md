> Source: https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:shop

## Description

The `shop=*` tag identifies retail locations selling products or services. It marks a place selling retail products or offering services, with the value specifying the principal goods or services sold. Approved for use on nodes and areas (including multipolygon relations).
