# Key: `surface` — Surface Material

> Source: https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:surface

## Description

The `surface=*` key describes the physical material covering a way, area, or feature. It is one of the primary landcover indicators alongside `natural=*` and `landuse=*`, and directly affects ground texture rendering. Applies to roads, paths, parking areas, squares, sports pitches, and any other mapped area.

## Element Types

- **Nodes / ways / areas**: may be used

## Status

De facto
