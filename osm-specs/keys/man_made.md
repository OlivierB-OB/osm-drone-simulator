# Key: `man_made` — Man Made

> Source: https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:man_made

## Description

Used to tag artificial structures other than buildings. The key covers a broad range of human-made constructions and objects such as towers, pipelines, masts, industrial equipment, and infrastructure features. It is used on nodes, ways, and areas depending on the nature of the structure.

## Values

### `man_made=adit`

- **Element types**: node
- **Description**: A type of entrance to an underground mine which is horizontal or nearly horizontal, as opposed to a vertical shaft.
- **Status**: de facto

### `man_made=antenna`

- **Element types**: node / way / area
- **Description**: An antenna to transmit and/or receive wireless signals, such as a radio or mobile telecommunications antenna.
- **Status**: de facto

### `man_made=beacon`

- **Element types**: node
- **Description**: A structure for signalling on land and sea, used as a navigational or warning aid.
- **Status**: de facto

### `man_made=breakwater`

- **Element types**: way / area
- **Description**: A man-made structure designed to protect a shore or harbour from the sea and waves, typically a wall or mound of rocks extending into the water.
- **Status**: de facto

### `man_made=bridge`

- **Status**: de facto

### `man_made=bunker_silo`
