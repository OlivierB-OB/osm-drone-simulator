# Key: `leisure` — Leisure

> Source: https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:leisure

## Description

Describes places where people go in their spare time for sport, recreation, relaxation, or entertainment. The key covers a wide range of facilities and natural areas, from formal sports venues to informal outdoor spaces. Most values apply to both nodes (point locations) and areas (polygons representing the full extent of the facility).

## Values

### `leisure=adult_gaming_centre`

- **Element types**: node / area
- **Description**: A venue with gambling machines and other pay-to-play games, with a set age limit restricting entry to adults.
- **Status**: de facto

### `leisure=amusement_arcade`

- **Element types**: node / area
- **Description**: A venue with pay-to-play games, such as video games, driving simulators, and pinball machines. No age restriction distinguishes it from an adult gaming centre.
- **Status**: de facto

### `leisure=bandstand`

- **Element types**: node / area
- **Description**: An open structure where musical bands can perform concerts, typically found in parks. Usually a roofed but open-sided structure.
- **Status**: de facto

### `leisure=beach_resort`

- **Element types**: node / area
- **Description**: Marks the boundary of a managed beach, where facilities such as sun loungers, umbrellas, and changing rooms may be provided and access may be controlled.
- **Status**: de facto

### `leisure=bird_hide`

- **Status**: de facto
