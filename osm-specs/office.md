# Key: `office` — Office

> Source: https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:office

## Description

The `office` key is used to map places of business where administrative or professional work is carried out. It covers a wide range of service-oriented offices. The key applies to nodes and areas (including multipolygon relations); it should not be used on plain ways or non-multipolygon relations.

- **Description**: An office for an accountant or accounting firm providing financial record-keeping, auditing, and tax services.
